# Project Status & Preservation Notes
**Created by:** MrBlight & NaniIsNano

## 📜 Scope & Intent
This repository contains the complete, unedited documentation of **Dunăreasc**, a constructed contact language and its accompanying alternate history universe. It is a creative, academic, and worldbuilding project. It is not intended for official, governmental, institutional, or commercial adoption—but nothing in this license prevents you from trying.

## 📂 Contents
All original text, tables, rules, lore passages, anthems, proverbs, neologisms, dialect notes, and register demonstrations have been preserved exactly as authored. No content has been summarized, cut, or altered from the original design documents.
