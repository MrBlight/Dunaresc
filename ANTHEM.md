**Created by:** MrBlight | **Collaboration:** NaniIsNano

## Raul cel Vešnic
The Eternal River — The national anthem of the Dunareasc people, sung at all official ceremonies. Written in a blend of Standard and High registers, with the Refrain deliberately in Popularu so every soul can shout it. The text memorializes the great campaigns of the legendary Dunareasc confederation — the wars of unification along the Middle Danube.

### Strofa I · Verse One
`Noi am pornit din šuma ši din piatra,`  
`Din Werk ši camp, din Haus ši rau hladna,`  
`Prin secole de sange ši de apa,`  
`Poporul nostru marea lupta sapa.`

We rose from forest and from stone,  
From Workshop and field, from House and cold river,  
Through centuries of blood and of water,  
Our people carve the great struggle.

### Refrenul · Refrain
`Dunarea curga! Dunarea curga!`  
`Sub cerul nostru, sub steaua noastra!`  
`Am cucerit! Am cucerit!`  
`Ši raul nostru numquam moritur!`

The Danube flows! The Danube flows!  
Under our sky, under our star!  
We have conquered! We have conquered!  
And our river never dies!

### Strofa II · The Verse of Campaigns
`Am cucerit Sarbii la campul mare,`  
`Steagul lor cazut — al nostru in soare!`  
`Am cucerit Grecii pe Dunare jos,`  
`Orašele lor — acum ale noastre, frumos!`  
`Am cucerit šuma, cetatea, ši marea,`  
`Pro Gloria — aceasta este calea!`

We conquered the Serbs on the great plain,  
Their banner fallen — ours in the sun!  
We conquered the Greeks on the lower Danube,  
Their cities — now ours, beautiful!  
We conquered the forest, the fortress, and the sea,  
For Glory — this is the way!

### Refrenul (Repeticie)
`Dunarea curga! Dunarea curga!…`

### Strofa III · Pro Miller Noge — The Fallen
`O, Miller Noge, tu ai cazut primul,`  
`La rau, in šuma, sub cerul ši fumul,`  
`Tu ai dat sangele pentru Patria noastra —`  
`Ši noi, cu Gott, razbunam in dimineaca!`

O, Miller Noge, you fell first,  
By the river, in the forest, under sky and smoke,  
You gave your blood for our Homeland —  
And we, with God, shall avenge at dawn!

### Strofa IV · Incheierea Magnus — High Register Close
`Sub Gott und Veritas, sub pax et lege,`  
`Pro Libertate toci sa se adune,`  
`Per saecula Dunareascul sa fie —`  
`Raul cel vešnic, lumina ši viaca!`

Under God and Truth, under peace and law,  
For Freedom let all gather,  
Through the ages let Danubian endure —  
The eternal river, the light and the life!

### Refrenul Final — Full Choir
`Dunarea curga! Dunarea curga! Sub cerul nostru, sub steaua noastra! Am cucerit! Am cucerit! Ši raul nostru — numquam moritur!`

---
**Note on the anthem's language:**  
Strofa I–II is Standart register; Strofa III mixes Standart with German-loan Gott for emotional weight; Strofa IV is Inalt/Magnus with Latin phrases; the Refren is pure Popularu — short, punchy Croatian-derived words — so that all citizens regardless of education can shout it at full voice.
