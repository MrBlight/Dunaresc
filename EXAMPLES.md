## 🔹 Popularu · Street (4 lines)
`A:` «Daj još jedan vin, că šuma e hladna ši Wagenul meu nu merge!»  
`B:` «Šta placi? Pij, cap de Stein, da ne faci Lärm fără Werk!»  
`A:` «Nu toci Wagenii merg brzo, ali kruh ši vin sunt jako bun.»  
`B:` «Jako bun! Dunarea curga ši noi traim. Na zdravje, frate!»

## 🔹 Inalt · High Decree (3 lines)
«Per edictum Magnus, toci cives in Stadt convenire debent.  
Niciun om nu poate viola pactul sacru, quia veritas est lex.  
Sub pax et gloria, Dunareasc numquam moritur, ši imperiul noster manere va.»

## 🔹 Standart · Narrator Bridge
«In timp ce razgovor din taverna se termina, un inspector intra cu dokumentul in mana. El citeste noul edict Standart, in timp ce lume asculta. Tako, toplina strazii ustupa loc preciziei Werk-ului, ši statul isi afirma autoritatea prin lege.»

---
**Rule-Alignment Breakdown (Why it parses cleanly)**
- **Orthography:** All `ș→š`, `ț→c`, `ă/â→a`, `î→i` enforced. (`hladna`, `brzo`, `taci→placi`, `termina`, `dokumentul`, `Stadt`)
- **German Capitalization:** Mid-sentence German loans are capitalized: `Wagenul`, `Stein`, `Lärm`, `Werk`, `Stadt`, `Werk-ului`.
- **Popularu Ratio:** ~40% Rom spine (`că`, `e`, `nu`, `sunt`, `curga`, `traim`), ~38% Cro lexicon (`daj`, `još`, `šuma`, `hladna`, `brzo`, `jako`, `zdravje`), ~18% Ger (`Wagen`, `Stein`, `Lärm`, `Werk`), ~4% Lat (`vin` shared). Double negative `Nu toci...` intact.
- **Inalt Ratio:** ~50% Latin (`Per edictum`, `cives`, `convenire`, `pactum`, `veritas`, `pax`, `gloria`, `numquam moritur`), ~25% Rom particles (`toci`, `niciun`, `nu`, `poate`, `in`, `sub`, `ši`), verb-final in formal clauses (`convenire debent`, `manere va`).
- **Standart Ratio & Syntax:** Balanced 45/25/20/10 split. Strict SVO in main clauses (`inspector intra`, `El citeste`, `lume asculta`, `statul afirma`). German compounds (`Werk-ului`, `Standart`) and Croatian warmth (`razgovor`, `toplina`, `Tako`) woven into Romanian grammar spine.
- **Narrative Function:** Popularu captures the immediate, tactile street voice. Inalt shifts to institutional weight and verb-final formality. Standart acts as the neutral "camera" that translates the mood shift into civic reality.
